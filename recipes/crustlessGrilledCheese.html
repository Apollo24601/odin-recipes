Crustless Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

These crustless grilled cheese sandwiches are great to have stashed in the freezer for when the kids need a quick snack. Easy for little hands to hold, with the cheese sealed inside and no tough crusts, theyâ€™re fun for them and convenient for you.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of white bread
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of softened butter

Directions

  1. Spread a thin layer of butter onto 1 side of each slice of bread
  2. Place a cheese round in the center of each slice. Top with remaining 4 bread slices, buttered side up.
  3. Center a sandwich sealer over a sandwich, press the sealer down, and hold it in place to seal the edges, 15 to 30 seconds.
  4. Place sandwich discs on a parchment-lined baking sheet and freeze for 2 hours before transferring into a freezer bag.
